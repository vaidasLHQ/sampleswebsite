## 1. Executive Summary

### Current State
SampleVault is a new e-commerce platform selling royalty-free audio sample packs inspired by trending Spotify songs. The site is currently deployed on Cloudflare Pages with a modern SolidJS/SolidStart stack. While the technical infrastructure is solid, the site lacks SEO optimization, proper page structure for search engines, and content depth needed to compete with established players.

| Area | Score | Priority |

| Page Structure | 3/10 | HIGH |
| Mobile Experience | 8/10 | LOW |
